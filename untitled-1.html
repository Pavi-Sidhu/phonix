Our Story

PhonixIT is a custom software development company that offers dedicated digital business solutions from small, to mid and large-scale enterprises. We have our offices located in the Melbourne and Adelaide. All the projects are in-house and are not outsourced. For 1+ year we built enterprise software and multiplatform applications that helped our clients better engage their customers and scale their business.
